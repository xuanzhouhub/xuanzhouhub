Xuan Zhou

Xuan Zhou is a Professor and a Vice Dean of the School of Data Science and Engineering at East China Normal University. Prior to joining ECNU in 2017, he was an associate professor at Renmin University of China, a scientist at CSIRO ICT Center (now renamed to Data61) of Australia, and a researcher at L3S Research Center of Germany. He obtained his Ph.D. from National University of Singapore in 2005 and his B.Sc. from Fudan University in 2001. Xuan Zhou's research interest lies in data management systems. He has extensive research and engineering experience on various database, data processing and data interaction systems.
